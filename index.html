ABOUT MANTRAS GREEN RESOURCES LTD.
Our Success Story
    The Company was established in the year 2005, in the name of Mantras Resources. The company is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company engaged in providing complete range of consultancy services for environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) studies and environment related projects. The Company has made rapid strides as a professionally managed organization under the leadership of our founder Dr. U. K. Sharma. Our sharp business acumen and visionary approach has placed The company among top notch organizations in the market. Our impact in the market is reflected by the fact that our versatile services are utilized in various sectors like Sugar Industries, Distilleries, Govt. Organizations and Co-operation Sector.
